The Marketing Funnel

Awareness

The first step in the marketing funnel where you make potential customers aware of your product or service.

Action Items:

  • Use SEO for organic website traffic.
  • Invest in paid advertising to reach wider audiences.

Interest

The stage where potential customers express interest in your product or service.

Action Items:

  • Offer educational content about your product or service.
  • Use email marketing to nurture potential customers.

Desire

The stage where potential customers express a desire to be associated with you and your products or services.

Action Items:

  • Offer free trials or demos to showcase your products.
  • Use testimonials and case studies to instill trust.

Action

The stage where the customer makes a purchase decision.

Action Items:

  • Provide a clear and easy path to purchase.
  • Offer excellent customer service to help finalize the decision.
